Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 25, 2025

Zipommy Dealers (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data we collect about you. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number when you submit a contact form or request a quote.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device type, and referral URLs collected automatically via cookies and server logs.
  • Usage data: pages visited, time spent on each page, and interactions with forms or buttons.
  • Communications: any messages you send to us through email, chat, or social media.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries, provide quotes, and deliver requested services.
  • To improve our website, products, and marketing efforts.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our site uses essential cookies for session management and analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand usage patterns. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, typically for up to 24 months after the last interaction, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal constraints).
  • Object to processing or request restriction of processing.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. International Transfers

We operate globally. If your data is transferred outside your country, we ensure appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them.
